[9. Stutiparivarta]

evaṃ carantu vidu paṇḍitu bodhisattvo saṅgā uchinnu vrajate jagatī asakto |sūryo va rāhugrahamukta virocamāno agnīva yukt
a tṛṇakāṣṭhavanaṃ dahāti || 9,1 ||

prakṛtīya śuddha pariśuddhimi sarvadharmāṃ prajñāya pāramita paśyati bodhisattvo |na ca paśyakaṃ labhati nāpi ca sarvadha
rmān eṣā sa prajñavarapāramitāya caryā || 9,2 ||

bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyāṃ stutiparivarto nāma navamaḥ ||
