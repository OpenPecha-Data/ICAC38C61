[13. Acintyaparivarta]

yo eva paśyati sa paśyati sarvadharmān sarvān amātyakiriyā ti upekṣya rājā |yāvanti buddhakriya dharmata śrāvakāṇāṃ prajñ
āya pāramita sarva karoti tāni || 13,1 ||

na ca rāja grāma vrajate na ca rājyarāṣṭrān sarvaṃ ca ādadati so viṣayātu āyam |na ca bodhisattva calate kvaci dharmatāyā
ṃ sarvāṃśca ādadati ye guṇa buddhadharme || 13,2 ||

bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyām acintyaparivarto nāma trayodaśamaḥ ||
